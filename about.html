Experience

Lenora cleaning company has been in business since 2006. Lenora has been cleaning building and homes for over 20 years. She worked for other companies before she decided to start her own business. Some of the places she cleaned are Agape church, Sandhill church, multiple homes, Jackson court house, and other business offices. She has cleaned them for years. They trust her to clean the entire building without them being in the building.
